Slow Cooker Red Beans and Rice

Pick Something Else

Description

Red beans and rice. This is a condensed version of a recipe found on The Food Network.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound andouille sausage
  • 1 smoked ham shank
  • 3 ribs celery
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 large green bell pepper
  • 4 scallions
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon brown sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 pound dried red beans
  • 8 cups cooked long-grain white rice, for serving

Steps

Prep Work

  • Chop andouille sausage, celery, onion, green bell pepper, scallions

Directions

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the beans, andouille, celery, ham shank, onions, bell peppers, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, cayenne, onion powder, paprika, brown sugar and 2 teaspoons salt
  2. Add the chicken stock and 2 cups water and stir to mix
  3. Cook on the high setting until the beans are tender, 6 to 8 hours
  4. Season with salt
  5. Serve with rice, topped with scallions
